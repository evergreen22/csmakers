Lessons & Materials

Welcome to the CS Makers lessons and materials site. This site is a repository of computer science lessons and resources from the NSF INCLUDES CS Makers grant and ALSDE Summer 2022 Teacher PD. The CS Makers curriculum is aligned with the Alabama course of study on Digital Literacy and Computer Science.

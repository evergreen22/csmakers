Lessons & Materials

Welcome to the CS Makers lessons and materials site. This site is a repository of computer science lessons and resources from the NSF INCLUDES CS Makers grant and ALSDE CS Makers Teacher PDs. The CS Makers curriculum is aligned with the Alabama course of study on Digital Literacy and Computer Science.
